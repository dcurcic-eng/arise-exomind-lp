The Science

What is Exomind?A workout for the mind.

Exomind delivers focused magnetic pulses to the dorsolateral prefrontal cortex - the region that regulates mood, focus and impulse control - using BTL's patented ExoTMS waveform.

Each painless pulse stimulates underactive neural circuits and harnesses neuroplasticity - your brain's natural ability to rewire itself. Session after session, healthy activity patterns are strengthened, like a workout for the mind.

The technology is FDA-cleared for depression, fully non-invasive, and requires no medication, no anesthesia, and no recovery time.
